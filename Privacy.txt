
Privacy Policy
News Reader for Google News (GNews) does not store, transmit, or parse any personal data.
Any webpage opened within GNews may have a different privacy policy, and GNews is not responsible for any
personal data collected, transmitted, or parsed by these sites.

Server Backend:
GNews uses a Google App Engine server to overcome limitations of cross-domain data fetching.
This server does NOT store any personal data, however request logging does happen and your IP address is stored.
This information is never used for any other reason except debugging the app, and will never be moved from secure servers.
